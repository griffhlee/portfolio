Tetragrammaton

Tetragrammaton is a long-form podcast hosted by Rick Rubin, where he engages in deep, thoughtful conversations with guests from various fields, including music, art, science, and spirituality. The podcast emphasizes exploration of personal growth, creativity, and the philosophies that shape people's lives and work. Rubin's relaxed, insightful approach allows for intimate, revealing discussions that go beyond typical interviews.

Song Exploder

Song Exploder is a podcast where musicians break down one of their songs, piece by piece, sharing the inspiration, process, and technical details behind it. Hosted by Hrishikesh Hirway, the show gives listeners a behind-the-scenes look at the songwriting and production process, offering intimate insights into how music is made. Artists from all genres participate, making it a unique exploration of creativity across the musical spectrum.

ReThinking

Hosted by organizational psychologist Adam Grant, ReThinking explores the power of changing your mind and reevaluating beliefs. Grant engages in conversations with experts, thinkers, and creators who have reconsidered fundamental ideas in their fields. The podcast encourages curiosity, open-mindedness, and intellectual humility, showcasing the value of rethinking traditional approaches in work, life, and society.

Huberman Lab

Huberman Lab is a science-based podcast hosted by Dr. Andrew Huberman, a neuroscientist at Stanford University. The show focuses on topics related to neuroscience, health, and human performance, breaking down complex scientific concepts in an accessible way. Huberman provides practical insights and actionable advice on topics like brain function, sleep, nutrition, and mental well-being, making the podcast a resource for those interested in optimizing their physical and mental health.
